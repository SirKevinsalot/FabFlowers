Do you work on more than one wedding at a time?

No. FabFlowers will only take one wedding per weekend to guarantee individual attention and decreased stress level for everyone involved.


Does FabFlowers make deliveries?

Yes. FabFlowers delivers to most of the Chicagoland area (additional charges apply).


Can you promise that my flowers will exactly match my colors?

No. Nature has a funny way of being unpredictable. If an exact flower is not available at the time of the event, the designer will use their best judgement in choosing a suitable replacement.


How much will my wedding flowers cost?

After the initial consultation an estimate will be sent to you. Flower prices vary by season. FabFlowrs strives to meet your needs and will work with any budget.
